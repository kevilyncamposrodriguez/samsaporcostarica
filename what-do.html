Samsa por Costa Rica is a non-governmental organization which emerged from the company
Corporación Grupo SAMSA de Centroamerica S.A.

The Samsa for Costa Rica project creates and trades digital assets (NFT), inspired by Costa Rican fauna in a vulnerable situation; for which it participates and develops directed activities with other organizations, such as the Zooave Animal Rescue Center, which promotes animal welfare.

The Samsa for Costa Rica project was born as an initiative from all the members of Corporación Grupo SAMSA de Centroamerica S.A.

Please find below a brief review of Corporación Grupo SAMSA de Centroamerica S.A.



During the 80s, in the mountains of Cerro de la Muerte in Cartago; Corporación Grupo SAMSA de Centroamérica S.A took its first steps, with the blackberry’s commerce. Thanks to its founder Juan José Quesada Garita. With the passage of time, and the acquisition of knowledge in the food industry, Juan José noticed that the industry demanded more variety of products, with the help of his wife and later with his children, they began a new path towards product diversification including the marketing of exotic fruits.

These products covered the needs that were required at that time, and since that moment on Corporación Grupo SAMSA de Centroamérica S.A became, until today, a pioneering and innovating company in the commercialization of high-quality products with accessible prices facilitating the consumers life.

Currently, Corporación Grupo SAMSA de Centroamérica S.A, is dedicated to the commercialization of tamarind in different presentations, production of fruit mashed for the industrial market, natural soft drinks and a wide variety of pulps, which include more than 24 flavors.



Corporate Social Responsibility is a principle that has been in place since our inception, since many years ago Mr. Juan José Quesada carried out different environmental initiatives, with activities such as planting trees with school children.

Likewise, there has always been a commitment to collaborate with donations to social welfare entities, such as Obras del Espíritu Santo, Casa Tzadic, Community Churches, among others. Academic training courses have been developed for the people of the community and there is full availability with educational institutions such as the Technological Institute of Costa Rica, so that students can learn more about the food industry.

In accordance with the above, in order to promote actions that promote Corporate Social Development (DSE) and to mitigate the environmental impact and promote social development, Samsa for Costa Rica emerged, which is a project that emerged to protect the animal welfare, natural resources and promote social development.



In the search to create strategic alliances, which would allow us to provide support to the proposed objectives, we have identified ourselves 100% with the Rescate Wildlife Rescue Center project, since, due to our roots, animal protection and its participation in the ecosystem is a main axis. Likewise, knowing the multiple requirements that they have for the care of animals, Samsa for Costa Rica decided to participate in the great challenges to make the dream of giving these animals a better quality of life come true, either in the shelter or in their natural habitat.

Rescate Wildlife Rescue Center (formerly Zooave) is a non-profit foundation dedicated to conserving the wildlife of Costa Rica. The Animal Sanctuary and Wildlife Rehabilitation Center is the oldest of its kind with 30 years of experience in rehabilitation, breeding of endangered species, habitat preservation and lifelong care of Animals that cannot survive on their own. Only in 2021 they received the amount of 7,000 animals for their attention and with the economic resources that can be obtained thanks to the visits of people to the park, there is really little to face the great demand for animals.
